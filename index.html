About me

Before this, I obtained a bachelor's degree in environmental engineering from Hunan University in 2017. Then I worked as an assistant researcher and assistant engineer for two years at the Environmental Science Research Institute of Shunde District, Guangdong Province.

I am very interested in research work that combines multiple disciplines under the framework of sustainable development, especially in combination with digital directions such as AI and ML.
